\chapter*{Abstract}

Language learning has traditionally suffered from a disconnect between classroom theory and real-world application, often leading to a "plateau effect" where intermediate learners struggle to achieve conversational fluency. This project introduces Badya, an innovative AI-powered language learning application designed to bridge this gap through immersive, context-aware practice. Moving beyond rote memorization, Badya leverages a multi-agent artificial intelligence architecture to create a personalized learning environment that integrates reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills.

The system features four distinct learning tracks: "Word Smith" for vocabulary building, "Story Weaver" for text-based comprehension and creation, "Echo Chamber" for auditory and pronunciation skills, and "Role Master" for interactive scenario-based roleplay. A key contribution of this work is the implementation of a parallel feedback system where specialized AI agents provide real-time, non-intrusive corrections and explanations, allowing learners to maintain flow while receiving detailed guidance. By dynamically adapting content to the learner's proficiency and interests, Badya offers a scalable, engaging solution that transforms passive study into active, practical communication, specifically targeting the needs of learners aiming for advanced fluency.
